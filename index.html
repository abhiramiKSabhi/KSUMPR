About Us

Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) is the official agency of the Government of Kerala, committed to empowering startups by offering robust PR and social media support. We specialize in helping emerging tech entrepreneurs amplify their presence, engage with target audiences, and showcase their innovative solutions effectively.

With a focus on strengthening Kerala’s startup ecosystem, KSUM provides end-to-end services designed to boost visibility, attract funding, and foster collaborations. Our PR and social media strategies are tailored to help startups build strong brand identities, share success stories, and connect with key industry stakeholders.

At KSUM, we believe in the power of communication and storytelling to propel startups toward success, and we are here to support every step of the way.

Benefits of Startups

What we offer to startups

Enhanced Visibility and Brand Awareness

Our PR and social media support can provide your business with the visibility and recognition it needs to succeed. By leveraging our resources, you can connect with potential investors, customers, and partners, and establish your brand as a leader in the market.

Credibility and Trust Building

By associating your startup with KSUM, you can benefit from our endorsement and gain recognition within the vibrant startup community. This can help you build trust with potential investors, customers, and partners, and establish your startup as a reliable and reputable player in the market.

Attracting Investors and Talent

By increasing your visibility through our PR and social media support, you can attract the attention of potential investors who are seeking innovative startups. Additionally, our platform can help you showcase your company's potential to talented individuals who are looking to join a dynamic team.

Services

What we do offer

PR and Media Relations

Media Outreach: Securing press coverage in local, national, and international media outlets.
Press Release Development: Creating compelling press releases to announce company news and achievements.

Social Media

Leveraging KSUM social media handles to publish content regarding your startup. Also, feature your startups in the Newsletter and other publications of KSUM.

Community Partner Program

The Community Partner Program connects Kerala's diverse communities, offering networking, KSUM resources, event collaborations, visibility, and funding opportunities for startups, makerspaces, and non-profits.
